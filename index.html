Popular Menu Items

  • Chicken McDo: A Filipino favorite, this crispy fried chicken is served with rice or fries.
  • Big Mac: The iconic burger featuring two beef patties, lettuce, cheese, pickles, onions, and special sauce.
  • Filipino Breakfast Meals: Includes options like Longganisa and Tocino served with rice and eggs.
  • McSpaghetti: A unique twist on spaghetti that includes sweet-style sauce topped with cheese.
